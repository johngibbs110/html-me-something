Road Trip!

In the summer of 2018, Cassie and I decided to leave our home of six years and move across the country.

San Francisco, CA

Our journey began in San Francisco. No, that's not our home below, just a snapshot of some classic row houses in the Bay.

Los Angeles, CA

We went staight down the 5 to stay in L.A. for a couple of nights. We splurged on a hotel in Koreatown, ate some great tacos, and saw some weird sculpture art near Venice Beach.

Las Vegas, NV

Next stop was Vegas, where it was triple digit degrees. My brother-in-law repelled down from the Stratosphere and I found some crack pie.

Flagstaff, AZ

The higher altitude and cooler temperature of Flagstaff brought much relief to everyone in the Green Machine. On our way into town, we made a brief pitstop at the Grand Canyon.

Santa Fe, NM

After a quick lunch in Albuquerque at Garcia's, we made our way to Santa Fe. The architecture, walkability, and food was divine.

Denver, CO

We continued north to Denver, where we linked up with some friends who took us to a Rockies game. Brad left his jacket and keys in his seat, so security let us in after hours to go look for it (to no avail). This is a snapshot of Coors Stadium after dark.

Kansas City, MO

The drive across the Great Plains nearly did us in, and we were in desperate need of replenishment once we reached Kansas City.

St. Louis, MO

Finally, after eight days of travel our '96 Honda rolled back into town with everyone intact!
